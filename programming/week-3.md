# Week 3: Future Living

**7 - 13 September 2026**

Week three turns toward how we might actually live together - architecturally, legally, ecologically, and socially.

---

## Theme

We explore cooperative housing concepts, map local resources and unused buildings, and examine the ecological potentials of the valley as a site for long-term habitation.

---

## Key Topics

- **Cooperative housing** - Legal structures, design patterns, governance
- **Eco-construction** - Sustainable building materials and methods
- **Land stewardship** - Community land trusts and similar models
- **Local resources** - Mapping what the valley offers
- **Regenerative systems** - Energy, water, food production

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Understanding of cooperative housing models
- Knowledge of sustainable building practices
- Framework for community land stewardship
- Skills in resource mapping

---

## Activities

- Workshops on housing design
- Field visits to local projects
- Mapping exercises
- Working groups on specific topics
