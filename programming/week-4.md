# Week 4: Governance and Funding

**14 - 20 September 2026**

The fourth week brings everything into the domain of stewardship: how we organize, decide, invest, and protect what we build together.

---

## Theme

We explore participatory governance frameworks, cooperative legal structures, long-term investment models, and mechanisms for holding shared assets in trust.

---

## Key Topics

- **Participatory governance** - Decision-making frameworks
- **Legal structures** - Cooperatives, associations, foundations
- **Investment models** - Crowdfunding, community bonds, regenerative finance
- **Asset locking** - Trusts, commons clauses, legal protections
- **Funding mechanisms** - Grants, sponsorships, revenue sharing

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Understanding of governance frameworks
- Knowledge of legal structures for commons
- Skills in participatory decision-making
- Framework for sustainable funding

---

## Activities

- Governance workshops
- Legal structure deep-dives
- Investment model simulations
- Working groups on specific topics
- Closing ceremony planning
