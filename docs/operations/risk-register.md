## Status Tags

* **Needs decision** - Cannot mitigate until a decision is made
* **Monitor** - Mitigation planned, watching for changes
* **Planned** - Mitigation steps defined, not yet executed
* **Accepted** - Low enough risk, no active mitigation needed
