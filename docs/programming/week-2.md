# Week 2: Cosmo-local Production and OVA

**31 August - 6 September 2026**

Week two shifts from macro-narratives to the mechanics of how a village economy could actually function.

---

## Theme

Through a mix of theory sessions and hands-on workshops, participants explore how global knowledge commons and local production capacities can sustain livelihoods, small-scale industry, and community resilience.

---

## Key Topics

- **Cosmo-local production** - Global knowledge meets local manufacturing
- **Open Value Accounting (OVA)** - Measuring and accounting for commons contributions
- **Toolkits and platforms** - Digital tools for collaborative production
- **Local supply chains** - Building resilient local economies

---

## Learning Outcomes

- Understanding of cosmo-local production models
- Practical skills in value accounting
- Knowledge of relevant tools and platforms
- Framework for building local production capacity

---

## Activities

- Hands-on workshops
- Field visits to local producers
- Working groups on specific topics
- Practical exercises in value accounting
