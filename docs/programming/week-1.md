# Week 1: Return of the Commons

**24 - 30 August 2026**

Week one sets the scene with a five-day course led by **Michel Bauwens** and **Adam Arvidsson**, two of the most insightful and engaging contemporary thinkers on the commons.

---

## Theme

This week explores the foundational concepts of the commons movement:
- History of commons
- Economic and sociological theory
- Contemporary examples and case studies

---

## Key Sessions

- Introduction to commons theory
- P2P and peer production
- Commons vs. capitalism
- Network societies
- Case studies from around the world

---

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of this week, participants will have:
- A solid understanding of commons theory
- Knowledge of successful commons initiatives
- Framework for analyzing existing systems
- Foundation for deeper exploration in following weeks

---

## Speakers

- **Michel Bauwens** - Founder of the P2P Foundation
- **Adam Arvidsson** - Sociologist and commons researcher
