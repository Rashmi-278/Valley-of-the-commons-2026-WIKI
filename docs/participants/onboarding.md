# Onboarding and Welcome Packet

- - -

## Pre-Event Onboarding

### One Month Before

* Welcome packet sent via email
* Final room assignment
* Roommate information (if applicable)
* Travel information
* Pre-orientation materials

### Two Weeks Before (for weekly visitors)

* Welcome packet customized for your week
* Schedule for your specific week

### One Week Before

* Final reminders
* Group chat introductions
* What to bring list

- - -

## What to Bring

### Essential Items

* Personal toiletries
* hiking shoes
* Layers for variable weather
* Swimwear for river dips
* Notebook for sessions
* Reusable water bottle
* Phone charger
* Any medications you need

### Recommended Items

* Laptop for sessions
* Camera for capturing memories
* Books to share
* Games to share

- - -

## House Rules

* Respect quiet hours
* Clean up after yourself
* Participate in kitchen rotations and other house teams
* Be respectful of other participants
* Follow safety guidelines

- - -

## Code of Conduct

All participants are expected to follow our code of conduct, which emphasizes:

* Respect for all participants
* Openness to different perspectives
* Non-violent communication
* Consent in all interactions
* Sustainability practices

Join our telegram group, which you will be invited to after ticket purchase.
