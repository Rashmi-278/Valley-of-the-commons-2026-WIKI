# Application and Acceptance Flow

- - -

## Who Should Apply

Valley of the Commons is designed for:

* Digital workers seeking intentional community
* nomads searching for a part-time base in nature
* Entrepreneurs exploring commons-based business models
* Activists and organizers working on social change
* Researchers and thinkers on commons topics
* Anyone interested in alternative ways of living

- - -

## Application Process

### Step 1: Submit Application

Fill out the application form with:

* Background information
* What you will bring to the community
* What you hope to take away
* Dietary restrictions and allergies
* Any special requirements

### Step 2: Review

Applications are reviewed by the team, considering:

* Fit with the community
* Skills and contributions
* Diversity of backgrounds

### Step 3: Acceptance

Accepted applicants receive:

* Payment link 
* Room preference form
* Group chat invite
* Virtual meeting schedule

### Step 4: Payment

* Pay ticket to secure your spot
* 70% Refunds till 15 July

### Step 5: Welcome Packet

One month before the event:

* Final room assignment
* Roommate information
* Travel information
* Pre-orientation materials
